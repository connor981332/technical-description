What is an SSD?

An SSD, or solid state drive is a storage medium used in computer hardware. What sets apart an SSD from other storage mediums, is it's complete lack of moving parts, hence the phrase "solid state".

The purpose of an SSD (or any other storage medium) is to serve as a place where your computer can store the information it needs to function. The performance and reliability of a computer's storage plays a big role in the entire computer' performance and reliability.
